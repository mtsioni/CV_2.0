Hobbies: Unleashing my Passion beyond Code

📌

When I'm not immersed in the world of coding and programming, I find solace and inspiration in a variety of hobbies that add a touch of flavor and joy to my life. These activities not only provide balance but also contribute to my personal growth and creative spirit.

Music has been a soulful and constant companion since the tender age of 5, and although my journey with the piano has taken an unexpected turn, through the ups and downs of life, its profound impact remains.
The melodies that flow from my fingertips, continue to serve as a powerful means of expression.
Music its always going be this one stable element in my life that sculpted me to the person and the adult I am today, through patience, perseverance and a gigantic will, it taught me the stoic path of understanding and living life.

It's a way for me to exercise my analytical skills and embrace the thrill of problem-solving beyond the realm of code, while at the same time it serves as a meditation tool for the days that I want to clear the fog I have in my head.
